Youth Climate Literacy: A 2025 National Snapshot

New research from Planet Ed reveals critical insights into teenage climate knowledge and misconceptions. Only 12% of teens feel they know "a lot" about climate change causes, and just 10% understand solutions well. This report provides essential data for educators working to improve climate literacy.
